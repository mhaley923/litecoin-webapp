About www.litecoincharts.com

Litecoin is a peer-to-peer digital currency designed to complement it's big brother Bitcoin and act as a faster, cheaper alternative. Litecoin (LTC) enables high speed transactions between anyone on the globe. According to the Litecoin foundation’s website, “The network is secured by mathematics and enables individuals to secure their own finances.” In other words, Litecoin is not under the control of a central government or bank.The coin was founded in 2011 by an ex google engineer, Charlie Lee. His mission was to minimize the slow transaction tim seen in Bitcoin. Fast forward eight years and litecoin's market cap is approaching $5 billion, with billions of dollars of transactions every single day.
